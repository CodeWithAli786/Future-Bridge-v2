Do you encourage and provide networking opportunities?

Yes, we as a co working space encourage and actually hope that anyone who works out of here gets to make the best connections in their field. We encourage curatives, professionals and entrepreneurs to network in and outside COLABS. Our office is set up as a community so you can naturally network and make relations that will last you and your business a lifetime.

Community
What if I’m not a member but I still want to attend the events?

We’re all about growing our community and adding as many people in our social events as possible. However, there are certain events that only members have access to. If you really want to attend, please sign up as a member and you’ll get access right away!

What type of people work at COLABS?

We have a varied assortment of professionals, creative’s, entrepreneurs and freelancers that work out of the co working space. We have people from different industries such as Tech/IT, Retail, Fashion, Textile, Finance, Marketing, Consulting, Legal and many more. We are sure to get you in touch with a wide variety of people from all sorts of backgrounds and careers!

What type of meeting rooms do you have and what do they include?

We have all kinds of meeting rooms for our members to conduct formal and informal meetings in. Meeting rooms include a bookable board room, conference rooms and meeting rooms. We also provide our members with multiple non-bookable meeting areas and huddle rooms for informal meetings.

For any further questions, please get in touch with us at info@colabs.pk

How do conference room credits work? How can i book a time for using a conference room?

Depending on their membership subscription, all members get a certain amount of complimentary access hours for bookable rooms. Bookings can be made through our online portal or the front desk reception.

What is Coworking?

Coworking spaces can be defined as membership-based workspaces where diverse groups of freelancers, small-medium sized companies and other independent professionals work individually or together in a shared communal setting. Coworking encourages innovation through collaboration.
